FREE-VARIABLES-EXAMPLES

examples pertaining to free variables in rules 
Major Section:  FREE-VARIABLES

The examples in the two sub-topics of this topic illustrate the handling of free variables in rules of class :rewrite (see free-variables-examples-rewrite]) and of class :forward-chaining (see free-variables-examples-forward-chaining]), respectively. These implicitly illustrate free-variable handling in rules of class :linear as well. Also see free-variables and see rule-classes.

  • FREE-VARIABLES-EXAMPLES-FORWARD-CHAINING -- examples pertaining to free variables in forward-chaining rules

  • FREE-VARIABLES-EXAMPLES-REWRITE -- examples pertaining to free variables in rewrite rules
