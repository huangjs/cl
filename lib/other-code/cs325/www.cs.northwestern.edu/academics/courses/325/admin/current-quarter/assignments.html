Assignments

You have two kinds of work to do. Individual exercises to build your Lisp skills, and project assignments. Assignments are simple tasks, with due dates. They role is to keep everyone in synch with the class discussions. Most of the assignments are not programming, but setting up pieces of Lisp software.

Both are required for this course.
